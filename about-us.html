Intel-Bot

Our technology enables us to scrape your website and produce a unique machine learning model based on the content of your website, creating a chatbot that you can use for lead generation, sales assistance, and customer support, among other things.

Our technology enables you to upload PDF files, XML files, YAML files, and CSV files in addition to scraping your website, so you end up with an original, one-of-a-kind product. Intel-Bot responds to queries in the manner that you choose. Additionally, our chatbots have SMS, WhatsApp, and Messenger integrations.

  • RTL assistance
  • Theme assistance
  • Customised chatbot
  • transferring files
  • comprehends and communicates in any language
  • SMS, WhatsApp, and Messenger integration
